Meet Our Tech Hero

Who is Megan J. Smith?

She is an American engineer and technologist who was the third Chief Technology Officer of the United States and Assistant to the President, (serving under President Barack Obama). She was previously a vice president at Google (not a CEO) where she co-created WomenTechmakers, and has used her platform in ethical ways.

Talk on the importance of diversity

Megan's Allyship

During her tenure as CTO, she worked to promote inclusive policies and initiatives aimed at increasing representation of women, minorities, and other underrepresented groups in STEM fields. She emphasized the importance of diverse perspectives in driving innovation and pushed for equitable access to technology and education. Smith also co-founded the Malala Fund, which supports girls' education globally, and has been involved in various organizations and initiatives that empower marginalized communities in tech.
